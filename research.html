ICE MC2 - Math Classroom Collaboration

Supervising professors: Dr.Pollanen and Dr.Kang

Goal of this project is to find a way to create a better online learning environment for math classrooms. There are a few focus areas of this study:

  • How math can be treated as a natural language instead of formal expressions.
  • How students perform with online office hours instead of traditional in-person office hours.
